Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Rasa ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

When you use Rasa, we may collect:

  • Account information: Email address and display name when you sign up
  • Health data: Food scans, nutrition logs, sleep logs, weight logs, and progress photos you choose to save
  • Usage data: How you interact with the app to improve our service

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Rasa health tracking service
  • To process your food scans using AI (OpenAI GPT-4 Vision)
  • To display your personal health dashboard and progress
  • To process payments if you subscribe to Premium

Data Security

Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use Supabase with Row Level Security, meaning only you can access your personal data. Our servers are hosted in Singapore for fast, secure access in Southeast Asia.

AI Processing

When you scan food, the image is sent to OpenAI's API for analysis. Images are processed in real-time and are not stored by OpenAI for training purposes. We use their API with data privacy protections enabled.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Database and authentication
  • OpenAI: AI food scanning and nutrition analysis
  • Stripe: Payment processing (we never see your card details)

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access all your personal data within the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Export your data upon request

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at hello@heyrasa.com.
